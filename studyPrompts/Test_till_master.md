# Test Till Master

> Share this prompt with any LLM (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.) after studying any topic.

---

I have just studied [topic]. Ask me 10 increasingly difficult questions to assess
what I understood. After each answer, give me a score and explain what I got wrong.
